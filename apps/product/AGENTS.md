# Vadal.ai — Stage 2 (the product, `apps/product`)

This app is **Stage 2** in the journey narrative, but functionally it is the Vadal product
front-end. Repo layout: `apps/` (deck = Stage 1 deck/frozen, product = this, hub = dashboard
on :3002), `brand/` (logo/mockups/avatars source), `design-system/`, `deliverables/`, `docs/`.
See `../../STRUCTURE.md`.

Stage 1 (`../deck`) was the design-foundation deck: explore directions, let the founders
choose. It is the frozen deliverable — do not edit it from here.

Stage 2 builds the **chosen** direction for real. Locked foundation:

- **Brand identity 01 "Signal"** — the V planted like a flag with a lens aperture carved
  through it and a four-point spark held in reserve.
- **Lumen** product-design style — warm, human, light + dark.
- **Positioning: Human pulse × Daily ritual** — keep companies human at scale, in an app
  people actually open every day.

Palette (Signal): periwinkle `#8DA2FF → #5D63E1`, signal red `#FB4B43`, white on near-black `#0A0A0C`.
